📖 About Gilli Danda

Traditional Indian Game

Gilli Danda is an ancient Indian street game that has been played for centuries. Players use a long wooden bat (the "Danda") to hit a small wooden piece (the "Gilli") and compete to achieve the greatest distance.

Game Features

  • 🎮 Realistic physics simulation
  • 🎨 Beautiful 3D graphics
  • 👥 Single player and multiplayer modes
